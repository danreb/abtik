
Using ABTIK STARTER theme

1. Copy the starter folder and paste it to your drupal themes folder "sites/all/themes"
2. Rename the folder that you copied to any name you want(example: mytheme)
3. Rename starter.info with the same name as your folder name(ex. mytheme.info)
4. Rename css/starter.css & js/starter.js base on the name you gave to your theme
5. Update your .info file with the new name of your .css and .js file
6. Start adding your css rule and js and design your subtheme
7. Change the logo.png and screenshot.png with your own
8. Enjoy!
